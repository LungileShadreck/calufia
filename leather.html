Inspiration

Calufia is inspired by a personal journey of resilience and creative freedom, transforming stories of self-expression into timeless designs. Rooted deeply in Botswana’s Tswana culture, it celebrates local leather craftsmanship and heritage, honoring the rich traditions and artisanal skills of the region.

Recognizing the growing global demand for authentic African luxury, Calufia aims to tap into this emerging market by building scalable, high-quality products that showcase Botswana’s unique creativity and economic potential on the world stage.

Origin Story

The Mmoloki Bag began as an experiment, a moment of creativity during my university days, when I took a burgundy corduroy dungaree dress and transformed it into something new. I sewed the bottom closed and wore it proudly. This was my first of many passion bag projects I made but never commercialized—but now it has become the soul of what Calufia stands for.

Why It Matters

Today, I am intentionally evolving that early design into Calufia’s signature flagship bag, The Mmoloki, meaning "The Liberator" in Setswana. It’s a tribute to where I started and where I’m going. It carries the story of a young woman turning grief into design, and creativity into structure.

Mmoloki

Noun | Setswana

Meaning: Liberator, savior, deliverer — one who sets others free from bondage, oppression, or difficulty.

/ mmō-ló-kī /

Pronunciation

The "Mm" is a nasal sound. Stress falls on the 'ló'.

"Mmoloki wa setšhaba o tlisa kagiso."
The liberator of the nation brings peace.
